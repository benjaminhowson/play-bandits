Play Bandits

Multi-Armed Bandit problems isolate the main challenge of sequential decision-making in uncertain environments. This challenge is the exploration-exploitation dilemma. Should I try the street food on Chapel Market? Or, should I have my usual wrap from Pret? This is an example of how the dilemma appears in my own life.

Luckily, there are provably efficient policies that optimally balance the trade-off between exploring lesser-known options and exploiting what you know is good.
